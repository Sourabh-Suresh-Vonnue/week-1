Health Drink

Preparation time: 10 min    Cooking time: 10 min    Total time: 20 min

Ingredients:

  • 1 Banana
  • 3-4 Dates
  • Nuts
  • 250 mL Milk
  • 1 tbsp Honey
    • Jaggery

Method:

  1. Boil the milk, wait some time to cool down.
  2. Soak nuts in water.
  3. Cut the banana and put it in a jar. Also add the socked nuts, milk and honey.
  4. mix/grind everying.
  5. put the drink in fridge to cool it down. (if dont like cold skip this step)

Your health drink is ready.

Terms glossary:

Boil
To heat up some liquid to its boiling point.
Soak
Put something in water until it absorbs some amount of water.

Make your own health drink and drink it to stay healthy.
