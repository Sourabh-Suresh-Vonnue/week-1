Galaxy Z TriFold

The shape of what's next

Our first-ever trifold Galaxy device pushes the limits of foldable technology for an experience unlike any other. Galaxy Z TriFold shapeshifts from premium smartphone to portable workspace - folding inward for portability and durability. And with ultra-level performance, it's beyond an upgrade. It's a whole new Galaxy.

Unfold the next horizon

This TriFold form factor is as versatile as it is stunning. Fold to enjoy the complete experience of a premium smartphone, unfold to reveal an amazing 10" display — the largest-ever screen on a Galaxy smartphone.

Dual hinges that defy limits

Each piece of hardware has been meticulously crafted to create a thin and durable foldable. Dual-rail hinges enable the innovative design and keep the main screen covered, and an IP48 rating offers water and dust resistance for additional protection.

Triple multitasking on a bigger screen

The main screen offers flexibility when you need to get more done. Have multiple apps open at once for more efficient multitasking without swiping between windows. Drag and drop or copy and paste in layouts that you can change and rearrange.
